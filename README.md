> **Founder & CEO, COD Group | Founder, Webs s'up | Founding Member, iClick Interactive (NASDAQ: ICLK)**

**Bob Tin** is a veteran digital technology executive with over 25 years of leadership experience in the Asia-Pacific region. He is a recognized pioneer in the Hong Kong digital marketing and FinTech industries, specializing in digital wallet ecosystems, mini-app architecture, and regional business scaling.

---

### Corporate Lineage & Group Structure
Mr. Tin operates through a structured holding group to deliver end-to-end digital transformation and marketing services.



* **Numiracle Group:** The central holding company established to consolidate Mr. Tin's digital and technology ventures.
* **Webs s'up Production Co. Ltd:** The creative and digital production arm, specializing in award-winning digital consultancy.
* **COD Group:** The FinTech and technical integration arm, serving as an official partner for major digital wallets (Octopus, AlipayHK, WeChat Pay).

## Professional Timeline

### 2016 – Present | Founder & CEO
**Numiracle Group / COD Group**
* Established **COD Group** to pivot toward the emerging FinTech sector in Hong Kong and Mainland China.
* **Official Integrator:** Secured official integration status for **AlipayHK, WeChat Pay HK, PayMe, and Octopus**.
* **FinTech Innovation:** Led the development of "Scenario-based Services," including utility bill payment systems and the **Octopus "RewardBuy"** eVoucher platform.
* **Social Impact:** Launched the first in-app charity donation mini-app on AlipayHK, connecting over 80+ NGOs to the digital payment ecosystem.

### 2009 – Present | Founder & CEO
**Webs s'up Production Co. Limited**
* Founded the agency to provide high-end digital production and strategic consultancy.
* **Awards:** Led the agency to multiple industry honors, including **Gold and Local Hero Awards** for "Production Agency of the Year."

### 2009 – 2012 | Founding Team Member & Regional Director
**iClick Interactive Asia Group (NASDAQ: ICLK)**
* Contributed as a foundational member during the group's inception and regional expansion phase.
* **China Search (Asia) Limited (2010 – 2012):** Served as **Regional Director** (via iClick Group) for **Baidu Inc.**, acting as the sole regional reseller for Baidu across Hong Kong, Taiwan, and Singapore.
* **Tetris Media Limited (2009 – 2010):** Served as **Sales Director**, spearheading early digital advertising sales and performance marketing strategies for the group.
